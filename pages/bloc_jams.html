Bloc Jams

A neat music player for tuning in and jamming out. Please see below for screenshots of this front-end project's layout and a brief overview of its features.

Built with AngularJS, Bloc Jams features functional media controls plus song duration and volume seek bars.

Bloc Jams is mobile responsive. No need to look any further if you're viewing this page vertically on your mobile device. If you're viewing this page on your desktop compouter, open it on your mobile device to see how things look.
